The Five Most Popular Games on Roblox in 2022

Roblox is one of the most popular video games of all time. With over a billion downloads and millions of players everyday. What makes Roblox different from all other video games is Roblox being a platform for thousands of games. Players and developers create games using the Roblox studio and publish them on the platform. Then other players can play these games online. In this list, you will see the Top five most popular games on Roblox in 2022.

5. DOORS

DOORS is a first-person horror game with the end objective of reaching Door 100 while avoiding entities that try to kill players. The game was created by the LSPLASH roblox group. Within the lobby, players can enter elevators that can consist of up from 1 to 4 players to explore The Hotel. Players start in the elevator. Once players exit the elevator, players will enter the Reception Room and receive a key from the billboard to unlock the door. The rest of the game is progressing by entering the following doors to enter the next room.

DOORS daily, has about fifty thousand players everyday and has about 1 billion visits this year, earning itself fifth place on this list.

4. Pet Simulator X

Pet Simulator X, is the third game of the Pet Simulator series. Pet Simulator X was created by BIG games. The game is about collecting coins and gems to unlock powerful pets. Coins can be used to hatch pets from eggs and purchase new biomes. New worlds will be unlocked as the player progresses, some of which introduce new game mechanics and features.

Pet Simulator X, has about 70,000 players daily and 3 billion visits this year, coming in fourth on this list.

3. Adopt Me!

Adopt Me! is a roleplay game developed by "Bethink" and "NewFissy". In Adopt Me! you build and customize a home, look after pets and make friends with other players. The main focus is on adopting, raising and collecting the various pets. Players can also pick between the role of a parent who adopts a child, or a child who can be adopted.

Adopt Me!, has about 200,000 players daily and 7 billion visits this year, making it third on this list.
