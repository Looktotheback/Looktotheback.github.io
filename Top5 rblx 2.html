The Top Five Most Popular games on Roblox in 2022 (Part 2)

2. Blox Fruits

Blox Fruits is a game inspired by the manga/anime series One Piece. Blox fruits was created by the Gamer Robot roblox group. The game is mostly aimed towards the Blox Fruits, which can be obtained by finding them randomly in the game. Blox fruits are items which grant different abilities to the user. Players can use blox fruits, swords, guns, and fighting styles to fight enemies, complete quests, and progress through the game.

Blox fruits, has about 300,000 players everyday and 6 billion visits this year, getting second place on this list.

1. Brookhaven RP

Brookhaven is a role-playing game with different buildings and locations that would be found in a town, including a store, church, school and playground. The game was created by "Wolfpaq", a roblox developer. Brookhaven features a large set of roleplaying items, vehicles, and homes. Brookhaven is known for its simplistic gameplay. Because of the game's simplistic gameplay, there is no in-game currency and players can unlock nearly all of the items as soon as they start.

Brookhaven RP, has about 400,000 players everyday and 9 billion visits this year, earning first place on this list.
